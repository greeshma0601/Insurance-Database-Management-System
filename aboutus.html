About Us


Insuranz Company Limited is a joint venture company. Insuranz Life combines Tata’s pre-eminent leadership position in India and AIA’s presence as the largest, independent listed pan-Asian life insurance group in the world spanning 18 markets in the Asia Pacific region. Insuranz Life has written retail new business weighted premium of INR 1,397 crores for the financial year 2018-19. For the same period, the 13th month persistency of the company was at 81.2% and, the retail claims settlement ratio was 98%..
